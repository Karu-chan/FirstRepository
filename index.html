Harrpy Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone is about this 11 year old boy who has been invited to the wizarding world and his rough beginning to life in this unknown magical place.

Major Characters

Harry Potter is our protagonist in this film. He's living with the Dursleys who don't treat him well. Harry's life changes completely once he visits the wizarding world.

Draco Malfoy comes from a wealthy family and is our first sort of antagonist. He enjoys making fun of others who are lower than him and breaking the rules just for the heck of it.

Ron Weasley, the youngest in his family, wants to achieve big things since he feels outdone by his older siblings. Ron and Harry become friends and help each other out whenever they can.
